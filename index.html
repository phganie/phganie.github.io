WHAT IS DIGITAL PRIVACY?

In the digital world, there is a potential risk of leaking your personal information while using the Internet and connected device. Digital privacy can be described as the protection of private information of individuals when using the Internet and any e-services.

Why does it matter?

"And right in the middle of all the complexity, your information is being routinely processed, stored and transmitted through global networks of connected systems." (Source)

WE need to be aware of the importance of protecting our own personal information. There are many cases that people are being fooled and manipulated by the hackers. Indeed, privacy is the need and right of each citizens.
